Who I'm I?

My name is Jose Juarez, I'm a Front-End Developer currently living in the Greater Toronto Area in Ontario Canada. Since I can remember my passion has been about creating art, I excelled at drawing and painting during my school years, then I found that I could create art using gimp and photoshop and make games using Unity following online tutorials, thanks to the many passionate people that write blogs and tutorials I've had the opportunity to enrich my knowledge in many areas of my interest, as well as to companies like Udacity, Coursera, Microsoft, Edx and Udemy where I have taken free courses about Html, css, JavaScript, Python and C# for beginners and intermediate levels, as well as courses about photography, film and acting. I've acquired my skills through online and self-driven education, and I strongly believe that both the present and the future will be a better world if we can bring more online education and training to anyone who aspires to be a productive member of society. If you want to know what I have been up to lately, take a look at my github!
